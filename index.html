About This Accelerator

What is IntelliSight?

IntelliSight is a pre-built AI analytics accelerator within the Xperion Marketplace — designed to turn raw business data into a decision-ready experience.

It combines conversational analytics, dashboarding, custom report generation, and recommendation logic in a single interface so business teams can move from data to action faster.

The result is faster analysis, sharper business visibility, and privacy-first execution without a heavy setup burden.
